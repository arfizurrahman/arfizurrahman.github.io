About Unika

Unika Service IVS was founded in 2018.

About the founder, Karim Siddique:
I was born and raised in Bangladesh and came to Denmark as a student at a college, where I met a Danish girl and therefore decided to stay. Today I am married to her and have two little girls.
I have worked my way up from scratch, where I started as a dishwasher and cleaning assistant. I have taken the basic course at the Hotel and Restaurant School and have since worked as a kitchen assistant and cook assistant for some years. In recent years, I have had the practical management responsibility for the dishwasher and kitchen assistant group in the catering company where I have been employed.
Now I am ready to stand on my own with my own company. My employees are happy to be employed, because they can feel that I know what the work entails.
